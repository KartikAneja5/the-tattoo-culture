Tattoo After Care

Preserve your art. Follow these steps for perfect healing.

01

Immediate Care

Remove the bandage within 1-3 hours of your session completion.

02

Gentle Cleansing

Wash your tattoo with antibacterial soap. Recommended: Dove or JOHNSON'S baby.

03

Apply Ointment

After 15 minutes of washing, apply a very small amount of ointment. Recommended: SS Suffa or Silverex Cream.

04

Daily Routine

Repeat steps 2 & 3 several times a day for the first 3 days.

05

Initial Transition

After the first 3-4 days, switch from ointment to a moisturizing lotion.

06

Continuous Hydration

Moisturize with Moiste several times daily for the next 2-3 weeks.

07

Strict Avoidance

Avoid direct sun exposure on your new tattoo. Strictly NO gym activities or swimming.

08

Long-term Preservation

Consistently use lotion and high-SPF sunblock to preserve the vibrancy of your tattoo.

09

Hands Off

Do NOT scratch or pick your tattoo while it is scabbing or peeling. Let it heal naturally.

10

Professional Advice

Listen to your Tattoo Artist's professional advice, not your friends!

If you don't take good care of your tattoo, you will be responsible for that and you have to pay double for touch up.
